Ingredients:
 - avocados
 - onions
 - cilantro
 - canned pickled jalapenos
 - garlic
 - tomatoes
 - salt
 - lime

Instructions:
1. Cut avocadoes into cubes
2. Chop onions
3. Chop cilantro
4. Chop jalapeno
5. Chop tomatoes
6. Squeeze limes
7. Add 2 tbsp of the jalapeno pickling juice
8. Add garlic
9. Mix everything with salt to taste
